Bus Features

Outside the Bus

Eco-friendly engines
Every Kidus Bus is equipped with the latest technology to burn fuel cleanly, reducing our impact on the environment.

Under-bus baggage compartment
You can store up to three bags under the bus (the first bag is free).

Outstanding safety record
We've received the highest possible safety rating from the Department of Transportation every year for more than ten years.

Wheelchair lift
All of our buses are equipped with a wheelchair lift to help customers with disabilities board the bus.

Inside the Bus

Wheelchair space
Each Kidus Bus can accommodate two passengers traveling in a wheelchair or mobility scooter.

Air conditioning
Air conditioning keeps the bus at a comfortable temperature, and individual air outlets are available at each seat.

Overhead storage
Put your carry-on baggage in the storage compartment above your seat to free up more room.

Choose your seat
You can choose your preferred seat when booking your ticket (additional fee may apply).

On-board restroom
Restrooms are available on all our buses.

At Your Seat

Individual power outlets
Nearly every seat has its own standard power outlet to keep your devices charged.

Extra legroom
Our buses provide ample space for you to stretch out and relax.

Free Wi-Fi
Stay connected with free Wi-Fi available on all our buses.

Reclining leather seats
Our comfortable leather seats are adjustable and have a handy footrest.

No middle seats
Everyone gets a window or an aisle seat, ensuring a more comfortable journey.
